<h3><strong>Overview</strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We’re on a mission to make the world a safer place with more secure software.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Cyber security is taking center stage for many companies, and the demand for Snyk’s product is sky-rocketing. Snyk has been adopted by over 2.2M developers who use Snyk to find and fix vulnerabilities in their open source libraries and container images, empowering them to develop secure software faster.</span></p>
<p><a href="https://snyk.io/news/snyk-acquires-fugue-enters-cloud-security-market/"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Snyk has now entered the Cloud Security Market</span></a><span style="font-weight: 400;"> and we are looking for a UX or Product Designer to design a range of experiences for users to easily find vulnerabilities in their cloud posture and fix them at the source: configuration code.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">You will be working closely with product and engineering teammates to help drive the future of our products.</span></p>
<h3><strong>You’ll spend your time:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Understanding the security analyst persona, what it's like to manage an organization's cloud infrastructure</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Working day to day in an R&amp;D team, and also collaborating with other designers&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Designing the system through user flows, design concepts and iterative user feedback sessions, to finally crafting hi-fidelity design artifacts</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Contributing to and using a design system to create a unified product experience</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Designing and conducting user testing sessions, both with Snyk stakeholders and users</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Conducting research to identify pain points and user needs to help influence product decisions</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Sharing your skills, helping to build our world class design team</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Making it easy for the 27 million developers in the world to build and ship secure code </span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As a company we are distributed across four offices in London, Tel Aviv, Boston and Ottawa, with 10% of the company working fully-remote.
