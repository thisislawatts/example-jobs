<h1><strong>Channel Manager, EMEA</strong></h1>
<p><strong>Location: London or UK (Remote)&nbsp;</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We’re looking for an entrepreneurial, driven and analytical channel development professional to work within the existing EMEA Channel Team to continue the momentum of building out the Snyk channel partner programme in the region.</span></p>
<h2><strong>Your role</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Working within the EMEA Channel Team, you will help build the Channel brand internally and promote Snyk and our capabilities to Partners externally.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">You will be responsible for managing relationships with a mix of existing Channel Partners, and working to identify and recruit new prospect Partners primarily in the UK.You will be expected to bundle our solution with other dev or security tools, resell via VARs or MSSPs, or integrate into cloud and digital transformation initiatives.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Among other things, you’ll spend your time:</span></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Recruiting channel partners on the program</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Structuring and defining plans with Tier 1 Partners in accordance with the Partner Program - to include KPIs, training/certification, marketing plans</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Executing with partners in collaboration with our sales teams to fulfil deals and build Partner-sourced pipeline, ultimately resulting in revenue</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Defining and growing the channel and cloud business with key partners</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Working with key partners to help develop their services portfolio to include&nbsp; Snyk solutions</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We’ll give you the freedom you need to thrive, including defining and achieving ambitious goals. Your work will influence our long-term growth and the scope of our future success.</span></p>
<h2><strong>You should join us if:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You can demonstrate a relationship-centric account management background and have direct experience in channel development and scaling, specifically in the software security space</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You have background in software security, open source, DevOps, cloud or dev tooling space</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You build meaningful relationships and love to find mutually beneficial opportunities</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You understand how large and small channel partners work, and how as a startup we can best partner with them</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You have a good understanding of Cloud Marketplaces</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You are process-oriented&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You are open, honest, outcome-driven and take an entrepreneurial approach</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You’re excited about the opportunity to build an effective, impactful channel programme!</span></li>
</ul>
