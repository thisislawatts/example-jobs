<h2><strong>Your role</strong></h2>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As a Support Incident Manager on our Customer Support team, you’ll be an escalation point to coordinate response for the most serious technical problems for our customers.&nbsp; The ideal candidate must be a self-motivated, strong team player with a background providing technical support for business-critical applications or infrastructure.&nbsp; You will work with the Customer Support Team and regularly collaborate with Customer Success, Sales Engineering, and our R&amp;D teams to help solve critical customer issues.&nbsp;</span></p>
<h3><strong>You'll spend your time:</strong></h3>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Acting as a focal point for high impact, enterprise account problem resolution.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Ensuring Support Incident Management processes are followed.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Providing accurate and timely customer updates and executive level communications.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Collaborating with multiple teams to make sure all necessary stakeholders are engaged to resolve an incident according to the scope and severity.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Recapping incident post mortems to document process deviations and areas for improvements.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Creating and maintaining playbooks for commonly occurring customer issues.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Contributing to building up a best-in-class technical support function, including documenting incidents and driving continuous service improvement.</span></li>
</ul>
<h2><strong>You should join us if:</strong></h2>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You can occasionally work outside of standard business hours to drive incident resolution</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You love helping people and care deeply about providing an excellent customer experience</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You take a logical approach to solving problems and prioritization.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You’re interested in security and you believe in our mission.</span></li>
</ul>
