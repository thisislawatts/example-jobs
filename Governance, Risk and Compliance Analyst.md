<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Advising on, documenting and tracking identified supplier risks</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Performing recurring assessments of existing suppliers</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Learning and collaborating on other areas of Enterprise GRC such as overall risk management, compliance programs as well as Business Continuity Planning &amp; Incident Management</span></li>
