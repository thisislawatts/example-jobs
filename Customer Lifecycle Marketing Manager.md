<p><strong>Customer Lifecycle Marketing Manager</strong></p>
<p><strong>Location: Boston, MA (Open to Remote)</strong></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Snyk is looking to welcome a Customer Lifecycle Marketing Manager who will be responsible for marketing programs that guide and delight customers through all phases of their Snyk journey.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">The Customer Success team works hand-in-hand with Development and Security leaders from modern dev shops to advance their open source security program using the Snyk Cloud Native Application Security Platform.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In this role, you will develop impact-driving marketing campaigns in partnership with many cross-functional teams across Marketing, Support, and Product Management. <p><strong>You’ll spend your time:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Creating, managing and tracking integrated, cross-channel campaigns across the lifecycle to guide customers to achieving success during and post-onboarding - driving usage &amp; feature adoption</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Developing a cohesive customer communication plan across channels</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Project managing customer engagements &amp; events in support of scaling customer success</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Operating as the organizer, manager, and leader of cross-functional projects</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Championing the effort to continuously advance the effectiveness of our customer marketing strategies</span></li>
</ul>

<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Are results and metrics-driven; you know how to measure and report out on marketing KPIs and performance.</span></li>
