<h3><strong>Overview</strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Snyk is looking for a Staff Product Manager to drive the direction and success of the overall developer experience within the Snyk Open Source product. With both software security and Open Source usage increasingly strategic and top of mind for organizations, they are demanding security solutions that not only provide risk coverage and mitigation but in an experience that feels like an extension of the everyday life of developers to ensure their adoption and success.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">As a Staff Level Product Manager, you will be among the leaders of Product Management at Snyk that will set direction, be a product expert to help execute on the most critical of priorities,&nbsp; engage as a community expert and help to coach and mentor the product managers of the team.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Your day to day responsibilities will include both long term strategic thinking and tactical day to day product activities.&nbsp; This includes ensuring you have a good perspective on market trends and opportunities, deep understanding of our users needs and workflows and translation of this into a roadmap and strategy that assures an experience within Snyk Open Source that aligns with the developers mental model on Open Source.&nbsp; You will also translate this strategy into roadmap, requirements and work with the engineering team as well as all your go to market counterparts to ensure a successful launch and overall adoption of features.This is a senior role within and across teams at Snyk including with individual engineers, product managers, developer advocates and with the Snyk leadership team where needed.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This is an exciting opportunity to lead the way on how developers work with Snyk Open Source and be that voice of the customer.</span></p>
<p><strong>Title</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">: Staff Product Manager, Product Management - Open Source Group</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Given our Global teams your regular workday must start no earlier than 9AM UK and no later than 9AM US/Eastern.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We’re looking for an experienced Staff Product Manager to help drive the direction of Snyk Open Source and the way developer-first security products, features and integrations are built by Snyk and our community of customers, partners and developers.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In this role, you’ll help define and execute on the product strategy for Snyk Open Source including working on the product strategy, KPIs and roadmap for Snyk Open Source and in particular around the developer experience and ensuring it’s aligned with the mental model of our user personnas.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Snyk has a positive, diverse, and supportive culture -- we look for curious, creative people that are ridiculously easy to work with.
