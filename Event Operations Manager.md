<h3><strong>Overview</strong></h3>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Are you passionate about the operational, analytical, and business relationships that make events successful? Are you hoping for a big career breakthrough in a company that is growing fiercely while also allowing you to focus on your own professional growth?&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">At Snyk we are looking for a driven, operationally-minded and collaborative event professional to support our growing global calendar of third party conferences and proprietary events in order to increase Snyk’s brand awareness, foster lead engagements, and create new sales opportunities. We’re on a mission to create </span><span style="font-weight: 400;">immersive experiences for our customers, users, prospects and the community at large - are you up for the adventure?</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">You’ll work closely across the marketing, events, marketing operations and procurement teams to help to identify the right tech stack and operational processes for Snyk to manage the pre, during and post event planning; rethink the user experience for both virtual and in person events; and report out regularly on business results.&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><strong>You’ll spend your time:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Driving events team success.</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> You’ll serve as a critical foundation and resource globally for both our third party events and proprietary events portfolios when it comes to execution - working closely with cross-functional teams to ensure we execute flawlessly for virtual and physical events.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Managing a multitude of events.</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> Responsible for standing up Snyk events operationally included but not limited to; resourcing across our tech stack, building Marketo and SFDC programs, contracting vendors, and ensuring for a seamless attendee experience.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Manage vendor relationships.</strong><span style="font-weight: 400;"> You will partner with vendors on execution towards the teams’ event goals. This includes swag, venue sourcing, attendee check-in, and upkeep of venue databases for ease of future event booking by key cities globally. Additionally, you will manage vendor relations across all global events including liaising internally with the procurement team on contracts and the AP team on all vendor payments.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Planning, budgeting, measuring, and tracking ROI.</strong> <span style="font-weight: 400;">You will work closely with event marketing managers to manage the overall global events budget against the priorities set for each event as well as coordinate closing out event budgets and payments</span><span style="font-weight: 400;">.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Scaling Snyk-hosted events. </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">Partnering closely with the Event Marketing Director, you’ll support execution against operational and system-related components of SnykCon and all other proprietary events.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Streamline swag. </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">Work with our global swag suppliers for sourcing, storage and fulfillment across all events and overall company needs.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><strong>Ideating. </strong><span style="font-weight: 400;">We strive to be the best event marketing team the tech space has seen. We’ll look to you to design engaging and transformative brand activations and experiences that provide purpose and engagement opportunity for attendees.</span></li>
</ul>
