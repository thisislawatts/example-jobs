<h3><em><strong><sub>All roles listed as ‘remote’ are available as remote within the same country.</sub></strong></em></h3>
<hr>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">We are looking for an enthusiastic and driven individual to join our Finance team to support Snyk’s hyper-growth as the Accounts Receivable Manager reporting into the Director, Revenue &amp; Commission. This individual should be highly motivated to learn and grow within the organization and show a proven track record to thrive successfully in a fast-paced environment. To sustain our explosive growth, we are looking for drivers, people who live for the next big challenge.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">You will be based in the Eastern time zone of the US, working either from the Boston office or remotely or a mixture of bothThis position is responsible for managing our Global Billings Teams. Functional responsibilities include management of all global customer billings, global AR metrics, reconciliations and month-end close tasks associated with AR and bad debt reserve and support the Global Controller in building scalable end-to-end billing processes for all entities. Additionally, this position will work closely with the Revenue Team in ensuring the billing process directly supports proper revenue recognition that complies with US GAAP (ASC 606) and local statutory requirements.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><strong>You’ll spend your time:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Managing all and scaling the current global Accounts Receivable team&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Assisting the Global Controller in the month-end close process ensuring effective and timely reporting according to the close calendar. Reviewing staff journal entries, account reconciliations and establishing and maintaining controls to ensure accurate reporting as it relates to Accounts Receivable.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Directly supporting the order to cash process by building strong relationships with sales operations, revenue, general ledger, and treasury teams.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Supporting the review of contracts to ensure proper documentation and billings are executed in a manner that directly supports Snyk’s revenue recognition policy (ASC 606).&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Compiling monthly global AR metrics to be incorporated into a monthly accounting review package which include financial metrics (e.g., DSO, aging analysis, analysis of payment terms) along with service level metrics (e.g., response time for customer inquiries) and a global assessment of the bad debt reserve</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Managing customer queries and escalations, while ensuring high customer satisfaction.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Managing the invoice templates to ensure proper taxation requirements are met for all entities (e.g., proper VAT Invoice in the UK, correct sales Tax in the US).</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Working closely with Revenue Team on ZoneBilling optimization and how data flows to ARM in NetSuite (“Advanced Revenue Management”).</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Working closely with the Field Finance Team on ensuring salesforce processes are optimized to support proper integration with ZoneBilling and other ancillary systems (e.g., Avalara, etc.)</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Creating process flowcharts, and developing, implementing, and automating internal controls and process improvements related to Accounts Receivable.&nbsp;</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">As Snyk grows and begins to get leverage from the NetSuite implementation, there is a potential for the collections process to be brought under this role in conjunction with the automation of the dunning process through ZoneBilling</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Being flexible in a constantly changing and high-growth business always looking to streamline processes and ensure Snyk is getting optimal leverage from systems and processes.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>You should apply if:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Bachelor’s degree, or equivalent, in accounting</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">5+ years in AR and 2+ years of management experience</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Solid understanding of US GAAP and general understanding of UK GAAP.</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Strong analytical and critical thinking skills</span></li>
</ul>
<p><strong>We’d especially love to hear from you if:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Easy to work with and can build strong relationships with a broad set of stakeholders that have diverse interests</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">Strong communications skills in both written and oral and treat communications with a sense of urgency</span></li>
<li style="font-weight: 400;"><span style="font-weight: 400;">You are able to juggle multiple priorities and tasks whilst remaining cheerful and resilient.</span></li>
</ul>
