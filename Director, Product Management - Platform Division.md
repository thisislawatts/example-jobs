<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">In this role, you’ll define the product strategy, KPIs and roadmap for Snyk’s Identity Platform.&nbsp; You’ll play a critical role in accelerating Snyk’s growth by leading the development of infrastructure that enables a standards based approach to authorization and authentication.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">Snyk has a positive, diverse, and supportive culture -- we look for curious, creative people that are ridiculously easy to work with.
