<p><span style="font-weight: 400;">This Business Analyst position will be a mix of responsibilities covering basic Salesforce administration functions and Territory Intelligence / Data Analysis. Responsibilities include taking a data-driven approach to Account Segmentation, Territory Design, Account Enrichment, Target Account Definitions, and Account Prioritization.
